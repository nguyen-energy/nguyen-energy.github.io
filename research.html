Revisited low-dimensional thermoelectric materials

We noticed Millie's very important historical work in 1993 (with Hicks) on low-dimensional thermometric materials. Simplicity in the theory of her papers has inspired us. There is a theory for that, and we know that low-dimensional materials are advantageous to bulk materials and they have superior thermoelectric properties. However, many people have tried that experimentally, and no one has been quite successful. Therefore, we want to know why and how to solve this issue. It is thus necessary to reevaluate the thermoelectric theory of the low-dimensional materials to find a new and general theory of the thermoelectric enhancement.
